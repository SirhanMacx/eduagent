# EDUagent — ProductHunt Launch Prep

## Tagline (60 chars max)
AI that generates lessons in your exact teaching voice

## Description (260 chars max)
Upload your lesson plans — EDUagent learns your style, tone, and strategies. Then it generates complete units, daily lessons, worksheets, and assessments that sound like you wrote them. 50-state standards. Works offline. Open source.

## First Comment — Maker's Story

Hey PH! I'm Jon — I taught for 9 years in New York City public schools. Every Sunday night I'd spend 3-4 hours planning lessons from scratch, and every AI tool I tried gave me generic output that sounded nothing like me.

So I built what I actually wanted: an AI that reads MY lesson plans, learns MY voice, and generates content that sounds like I wrote it. Not "a teacher" — ME.

What makes this different:
- It ingests YOUR files (PDFs, DOCX, PPTX) and extracts your teaching persona
- Every lesson matches your tone, vocabulary, and preferred structure
- Auto-aligns to your state's standards (CCSS, NGSS, TEKS, all 50 states)
- Students get a chatbot that answers questions in your teaching voice
- Runs locally with Ollama (free) or with Anthropic/OpenAI

After 9 years of lesson planning, this is the tool I wish I'd had on day one. It's MIT-licensed and free forever.

Would love your feedback — especially from any teachers in the community!
