## Description (260 chars for PH)

EDUagent ingests your existing curriculum files and learns your teaching voice. Then it generates complete lesson plans, worksheets, DBQs, and assessments — in your exact style. Works with Anthropic, OpenAI, or free local Ollama. Open source, MIT licensed.
