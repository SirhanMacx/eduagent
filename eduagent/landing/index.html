How it works

Install

pip install eduagent — one command, works on Mac, Linux, and Windows. No account needed.

Ingest

Point it at your lesson folder. EDUagent reads PDFs, DOCX, PPTX, even SMART Board files — and extracts your teaching persona.

Generate

Ask for a lesson, a unit, a worksheet, or a full course. Everything comes out in your voice, aligned to your state standards.
