# EDUagent — 60-Second Demo Script

**For:** Jon recording on his phone, propped up showing the terminal.
**Goal:** Show a real teacher using EDUagent in under a minute.

---

## Setup Before Recording

1. Open terminal, make the font big (Cmd-+ a few times)
2. Clear the screen: `clear`
3. Make sure EDUagent is installed and your materials are ingested

---

## The Script

### [0:00] Start recording. Terminal is visible.

**Type:**

```
eduagent chat
```

*Wait for the welcome panel to appear.*

### [0:05] Say out loud:

> "This is EDUagent. I built it because I got tired of spending my Sundays writing lesson plans from scratch."

### [0:10] Type:

```
I teach 10th grade Global History at Great Neck South
```

*EDUagent responds with your teaching profile — inquiry-based, AIM questions, primary sources. Let it sit on screen for 2 seconds.*

### [0:20] Say:

> "It already knows my style. I fed it 9 years of my lesson plans."

### [0:25] Type:

```
plan a lesson on the causes of World War I
```

*Wait for the lesson to generate. It should show: AIM question, Do Now, document analysis activity, exit ticket.*

### [0:35] Scroll slowly so the camera catches:

- The AIM question (e.g., "Was World War I inevitable, or could it have been prevented?")
- The Do Now
- The document analysis activity
- The exit ticket

### [0:42] Say:

> "AIM question, Do Now, document analysis — that's my format. Not a generic template. Mine."

### [0:47] Type:

```
make a worksheet
```

*Let it generate. Brief pause.*

### [0:52] Say:

> "This is my teaching style. EDUagent learned it from 9 years of my lesson plans. It's free and open source."

### [0:58] Say:

> "Link in bio. pip install eduagent."

### [1:00] Stop recording.

---

## Tips

- Don't rehearse too much — real > polished
- If you mess up a word, keep going. Teachers are human.
- Vertical video (phone) is fine for Twitter/TikTok. Horizontal for YouTube.
- Natural lighting, no ring light needed. You're a teacher, not an influencer.
