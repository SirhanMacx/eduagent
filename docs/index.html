The Causes of World War II: From Appeasement to Invasion

Grade 10 · US History · 55 minutes · CCSS.RH.9-10.2, CCSS.RH.9-10.6
Do Now (5 min)

Examine the political cartoon (Neville Chamberlain, "Peace for our time," 1938). In 2–3 sentences: What is the cartoonist arguing? What evidence in the image supports your claim?

Guided Notes
  • Treaty of Versailles — economic conditions that fueled resentment
  • Rise of totalitarianism: compare Hitler, Mussolini, and Tojo
  • Appeasement policy — Munich Agreement (1938), why Britain and France chose it
  • Invasion of Poland (September 1, 1939) — the breaking point
  • US neutrality vs. Lend-Lease — shifting American opinion
  • Pearl Harbor — from isolation to total war
Exit Ticket

1. Identify TWO causes of WWII and explain how they are connected. 2. Was appeasement a reasonable strategy in 1938? Defend your position with evidence. 3. How did American policy shift between 1939 and 1941?

Get Started

Up and running in three commands

Only to talk to your AI provider (like any chatbot). Your curriculum files are processed and stored entirely on your machine. If you use a local model through Ollama with Llama 4, everything runs fully offline with complete privacy.
Every subject. History, Science, Math, ELA, AP courses, Art, Music, World Languages, CTE, Computer Science — Claw-ED learns from YOUR files, so it adapts to whatever you teach. It includes standards databases covering all 50 US states (CCSS, NGSS, C3, and state-specific standards).
When you run clawed ingest, it reads your lesson plans, worksheets, and assessments. It chunks and embeds every document, extracting your vocabulary, structure patterns, how you frame essential questions, and your overall pedagogical style. Every lesson it generates uses those patterns. The more files you feed it, the better it sounds like you.
Your files never leave your computer. Claw-ED processes and stores everything locally in a semantic database on your machine. The only external call is to your chosen AI provider for text generation — and you pick that provider. There are no accounts, no telemetry, no data collection, no analytics. The code is open source so you can verify this yourself.
